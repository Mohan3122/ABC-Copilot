# Mutual Fund Knowledge Copilot

## Architecture of the Unified Agent

The unified agent should orchestrate these 4 internal specialized agents:

| Internal Agent | Purpose | Primary Users |
|---------------|----------|--------------|
| Fund Intelligence Agent | Fund information, product knowledge, scheme comparisons | Sales Teams, Product Teams, Relationship Managers |
| Sales & Distributor Enablement Agent | Distributor support, sales pitches, FAQs, positioning | Sales Teams, Channel Managers, RM Teams |
| Compliance & Governance Agent | Regulatory guidance, compliance policies, approved communications | Compliance Teams, Sales Teams, Product Teams |
| RFP & Research Intelligence Agent | Institutional RFPs, DDQs, research retrieval, meeting intelligence | Institutional Sales, Product Teams, Investment Teams |

👉 The user interacts with **ONE unified agent**

👉 Internally, the agent intelligently chooses the right capability

👉 The agent uses only Microsoft 365 knowledge sources

---

## Why These 4 Agents?

These 4 capabilities cover nearly **80–90% of knowledge-intensive activities** across a mutual fund organization:

1. Fund information & product intelligence
2. Sales enablement & distributor support
3. Compliance & governance guidance
4. RFP preparation, research retrieval & meeting insights

---

# Unified Agent Responsibilities

## 1. Fund Information & Product Intelligence

### Fund Knowledge Support

* Compare mutual fund schemes
* Explain investment objectives and strategies
* Summarize fund mandates
* Retrieve fund positioning documents
* Explain asset allocation approaches
* Compare risk-return characteristics
* Surface historical product updates

### Product Intelligence

* Retrieve latest fund factsheets
* Summarize Scheme Information Documents (SIDs)
* Explain investment themes and market outlooks
* Identify suitable schemes based on documented product positioning

### Output

* Fund comparison summaries
* Product insights
* Investment strategy summaries
* Scheme positioning recommendations
* Product documentation references

---

## 2. Sales & Distributor Enablement

### Sales Support

* Generate sales talking points
* Create meeting preparation briefs
* Retrieve approved product messaging
* Summarize product differentiators
* Surface distributor FAQs
* Recommend relevant sales collateral

### Distributor Support

* Answer distributor queries using approved content
* Provide onboarding guidance
* Retrieve distributor communication guidelines
* Surface approved responses to common objections

### Meeting Preparation

* Generate client or distributor meeting briefs
* Summarize recent product developments
* Compile relevant sales material

### Output

* Sales talking points
* Distributor-ready responses
* Meeting preparation packs
* Product positioning summaries
* Approved marketing messages

---

## 3. Review Policies, Compliance & Governance

### Compliance Support

* Search compliance policies and procedures
* Explain regulatory guidelines from approved documents
* Retrieve communication approval requirements
* Surface audit observations and governance standards
* Identify policy conflicts or outdated references

### Communication Validation

* Verify whether messaging aligns with approved communication guidelines
* Highlight compliance-related considerations
* Reference governing policies and procedures

### Governance Knowledge

* Retrieve investment governance framework
* Summarize committee decisions
* Explain escalation and approval processes

### Output

* Policy references
* Compliance guidance
* Governance summaries
* Regulatory document citations
* Communication review insights

---

## 4. Support RFPs, DDQs & Research Retrieval

### RFP & Due Diligence Support

* Search historical RFP responses
* Retrieve previously approved answers
* Draft responses using existing approved content
* Summarize governance and investment processes
* Compile supporting documentation

### Investment Research Support

* Search analyst reports
* Retrieve sector research
* Summarize investment committee viewpoints
* Surface historical investment themes
* Compare research perspectives

### Meeting Intelligence

* Analyze Teams meeting transcripts
* Summarize committee discussions
* Identify decisions and action items
* Generate executive summaries

### Output

* RFP draft responses
* DDQ responses
* Research summaries
* Committee decision summaries
* Executive briefing notes
